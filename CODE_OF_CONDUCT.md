# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in WhyCache welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of technical expertise.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and keep discussions focused on the project. Do not harass, insult, threaten, publish private information, or disrupt collaboration.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainer through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project in public spaces.
